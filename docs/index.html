About FoodLodge

FoodLodge is a free mobile app that connects individuals and businesses with surplus food to people in need, reducing food waste and supporting communities across London.

Donors post surplus food listings with photos and pick-up times. Recipients browse a live map, claim what they need, and collect for free.
