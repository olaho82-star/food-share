# Privacy Policy — FoodLodge

**Last updated: May 2026**

FoodLodge ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information you provide
- **Account details**: your name and email address when you register
- **Profile information**: your London borough and role (Donor or Recipient)
- **Listings**: food photos, descriptions, and pick-up addresses you submit as a Donor
- **Messages**: in-app messages you send to other users
- **Payment information**: if you make a voluntary donation or subscribe to FoodLodge Business, payment details are processed by Stripe and are never stored on our servers

### Information collected automatically
- **Location**: approximate location to show you nearby food listings (only when you grant permission)
- **Push notification token**: to send you alerts about listings, messages and collections
- **Usage data**: basic app activity to improve performance and fix issues

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide the FoodLodge service and connect donors with recipients
- Send push notifications about listings, messages and collection confirmations
- Display food listings on the map (borough-level only — full address is only revealed to the recipient who claims a listing)
- Process voluntary donations and Business subscriptions via Stripe
- Improve the app and troubleshoot issues
- Ensure the safety and integrity of the platform

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties.

We share data only with:
- **Firebase** (Google): for real-time messaging and push notifications
- **Stripe**: for processing payments (voluntary donations and Business subscriptions)
- **MongoDB Atlas**: for secure database storage
- **Render**: for hosting our server infrastructure

All third-party services are bound by their own privacy policies and applicable data protection law.

---

## 4. Address Privacy

Your full pick-up address is **never** shown publicly. It is only revealed to a recipient after they have claimed your listing. All public-facing views show borough-level location only.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Food listings and anonymised exchange records may be retained for statistical purposes.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Withdraw consent for push notifications at any time (via device settings)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **hello@foodlodge.app**

---

## 7. Children

FoodLodge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of FoodLodge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
