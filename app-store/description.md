## Description (max 4000 chars)

FoodLodge connects people and businesses with surplus food to neighbours who need it — completely free, right across London.

**For Donors**
Have leftover food from an event, a restaurant, or simply a full fridge? Post a listing in seconds. Set your pick-up window, add a photo, and let your community know what's available. When someone claims your food, you'll get notified instantly. Every donation helps reduce food waste and supports people in your area.

**For Recipients**
Browse food listings on a live map or list view, filtered by category. Claim a listing you want, message the donor directly, and collect at the agreed time. Everything is free — no fees, no subscriptions.

**How it works**
1. Sign up as a Donor or Recipient
2. Donors post surplus food with a photo, pick-up time and location
3. Recipients browse the map and claim what they need
4. Both sides confirm collection — exchange complete

**Key features**
• Live map of available food near you
• Real-time push notifications
• In-app messaging with every donor and recipient
• Food safety reminders on every listing
• Voluntary thank-you donations from recipients to donors
• Borough-level privacy — full address only revealed after claiming
• Two-way collection confirmation

**For Businesses**
Restaurants, cafes, supermarkets and caterers can upgrade to FoodLodge Business for priority listing placement and a verified badge — helping you reach more recipients faster while demonstrating your commitment to sustainability.

**Our mission**
Tonnes of good food are thrown away across London every day while many families struggle to put meals on the table. FoodLodge exists to close that gap — one listing at a time.

Join the FoodLodge community and help make London a little less wasteful.
